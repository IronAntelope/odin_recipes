Momos

Description

Steamed dumplings originating from Nepal and Tibet that are popular in Bhutan and India. Served with a sauce known as achar.

Ingredients

    Dough

  • all-purpose flour
  • cooking oil
  • water as required
  • salt

    Filling

  • red onion
  • green onion
  • tomatoes
  • coriander
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • nutmeg
  • turmeric
  • curry powder
  • red chillies
  • cooking oil, salt and pepper

    Achar

  • tomatoes
  • onion
  • ginger-garlic paste
  • serrano pepper
  • red chilli powder
  • cumin-coriander powder
  • oil
  • turmeric powder

Steps

    Dough and Filling

  1. in a large bowl, combine flour, oil, salt and water
  2. mix and knead for 8-10 minutes
  3. cover and stand for 30 minutes then knead again
  4. in a large bowl, combine all filling ingredients and mix well
  5. cover and refrigerate for an hour

    Achar

  1. heat oil and fry onion until brown
  2. add serrano pepper and cook for 1 minute
  3. add all other ingredients except for coriander and cook until a curry consistency
  4. add coriander and stir 2-3 times
  5. blend all together in a blender

    Assembly

  1. knead the dough a final time
  2. make 1 inch dough balls
  3. roll balls to smooth spheres
  4. dust working board with flour
  5. on board, gently flatten ball with palm to about 2 inch circle
  6. with a rolling pin, flatten circles into wrappers
  7. thin the edges with fingers till wrappers are 3 inches in diameter

    8. Wrapping

  8. put one tablespoon of filling in the wrapping
  9. bring edges to the center, pinch and twist
  10. heat up steamer and oil steamer racks
  11. place momos in the steamer
  12. close lid and cook for about 10 minutes
  13. take momos off the steamer and serve with with achar
