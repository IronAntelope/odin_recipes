Lasagna

Description

An Italian dish with layers of pasta sheets and various fillings including beef mince and multiple diced veggies topped with a thick creamy white sauce

Ingredients

Meat Sauce

  • beef mince
  • onion
  • garlic
  • carrot
  • celery
  • soffritto
  • canned tomato and tomato paste
  • red wine (optional for flavour)
  • seasonings - stock cubes, bay leaves, thyme, oregano, worcestershire sauce

White Sauce

  • butter
  • flour
  • milk
  • cheese

For Assembling

  • pasta sheets
  • cheese

Steps

  1. smear some of the meat sauce on the base under the first layer
  2. add the first layer and top with meat sauce plus some white sauce
  3. add next layer then meat sauce and white sauce until dish is full
  4. top with remaining white sauce and cheese
